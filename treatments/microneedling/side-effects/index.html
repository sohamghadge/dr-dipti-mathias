Microneedling & MNRF — Side Effects & Safety Profile

Microneedling Radio Frequency (MNRF) to rebuild collagen, refine skin texture, and smooth acne scars.

Overview of Microneedling & MNRF

Microneedling Radio Frequency (MNRF) to rebuild collagen, refine skin texture, and smooth acne scars. Under the care of Dr. Dipti Mathias, each protocol is customized to deliver natural results.

Clinical Safety & Post-Care

Fine insulated needles penetrate the skin and deliver radiofrequency energy, heating the dermis. Safety is our highest priority at Good Genes Dermatology. Common mild reactions include brief redness or swelling.

Contraindications: History of keloids, active eczema or psoriasis, or active inflammatory acne.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Microneedling & MNRF procedure take?

A standard session of Microneedling & MNRF at our Khar West clinic typically takes about 30 to 45 minutes of active treatment, though we suggest planning for an hour to complete consultation and preparation.

Is Microneedling & MNRF painful?

Most patients describe the sensation as a mild pinch or warmth. For needle-based or energy treatments, we apply a premium topical numbing cream for 30-45 minutes beforehand to ensure absolute comfort.

What is the cost of Microneedling & MNRF in Mumbai?

The cost for Microneedling & MNRF typically ranges from ₹12,000 - ₹25,000 per session. The exact pricing depends on the surface area, severity of the condition, and number of sessions required.

Are there any side effects of Microneedling & MNRF?

Mild redness, slight swelling, or temporary tenderness are normal and resolve within a few hours to a couple of days. We provide comprehensive post-care guidelines to support healing.

Who is the ideal candidate for this treatment?

This procedure is ideal for patients looking to address specific cosmetic or clinical skin issues. However, History of keloids, active eczema or psoriasis, or active inflammatory acne. should be avoided.
