— About the Doctor

The Science of Precision &
Cellular Longevity.

Dr. Dipti Mathias is a board-certified Aesthetic Dermatologist and Trichologist with eight years of clinical experience — five of them as a specialist. She completed her MBBS at Fr. Muller Medical College, Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (2017), followed by an MD in Dermatology, Venereology & Leprosy at Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Pune (2021).

Her career moves with intention rather than volume — tenures at Kaya Limited, MakeOskin, and The Eterne Clinic refined a single conviction: that skin is a long-term science, not a seasonal trend. In 2024 she established Good Genes Dermatology as Mumbai’s newest premium single-doctor boutique — one chair, one doctor, one uncompromising standard.

Structural Rejuvenation & Lifting

  • Skin lifting and tightening — Non-invasive tissue tightening and structural tightening protocols.
  • HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) — Targeted deep-layer SMAS lifting to address laxity.
  • Facelift without surgery — Advanced liquid and energy-based contour suspension.
  • Botox & Dysport — Micro-dosing for smoothing fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Fillers & Facial contouring — Restoring structural volume and jawline definition.

Regenerative Aesthetics & Skin Quality

  • Salmon DNA / PDRN — Advanced cellular healing and tissue regeneration.
  • Microneedling & MNRF — Microneedling radio frequency to rebuild collagen.
  • Pico laser — Precision picosecond photo-acoustic pigment shattering.
  • Chemical peels — Multi-layered chemical exfoliation for uneven skin tone.
  • Medical Facials — Clinical recovery and cellular nourishment protocols.

Targeted Corrective Procedures

  • Acne & Acne scars — Subcision, chemical reconstruction, and laser resurfacing.
  • Dark circles — Under-eye vascular correction and structural hollow support.
  • Cautery for Skintags — Safe ablation of tags, milia, and benign skin lesions.
